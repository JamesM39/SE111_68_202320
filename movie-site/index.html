About Firefly

In the far future humanity has left Earth and terraformed a couple dozen planets. However wealth distribution remains as bad as ever, as a handfull of planets called core worlds enjoy advanced technology and wealth while all the other planets have devolved into the wild west, where bandits are common and most people struggle to get by. We follow ex-soldier Malcolm Reynalds, captain of the Firefly class ship Serenity and its crew of misfits, as they do what they can to keep flying.

Produced by: Mutant Enemy Productions & 20th Century Fox Television

Released: September 20, 2002 - December 20, 2002

Genres: Sci-fi, Western

Rating: TV-14

Number of Episodes 14
